SWIP is an application that aims to reduce cash receipts cluttering our purses.
This professional school project allows us to understand the financial and legal issues of a start-up. Indeed, from its conceptualization to the realization, we have been involved in all stages of the process. As in charge of the graphic creation, I created for SWIP an interactive mobile design as well as illustrations for the tutorials. The graphic charter is based on two watchwords: pop and fun! We wished that its use is the simplest and pleasant possible to retain our users. SWIP is still unreleased but you will find here some images of the mock-ups.
