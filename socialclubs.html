Mindfulness-Social-Club is a website that I fully realized as part of an internship during my first year degree.
This 7 weeks work placement had as main tasks the making of a website and the set up of a communication strategy on social networks. The company I worked for is specialized in mindfulness meditation and wanted to start a new stage in their project. Therefore I created the design of the website after having participated in the creation of the brand identity. The website that is currently online slightly differs from the proposed models because it has been integrated from an existing Wordpress theme.
