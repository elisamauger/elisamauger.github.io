Combining my two passions, electronic music and digital to create Drywet's communication.
Drywet is a Bordeaux based association that organises events and nights around electronic music. Seeking a volunteer to create their digital communication, I proposed myself in order to develop my skills in this field outside the course projects. I was able to create a social network strategy for them and other cool projects such as teasers, visuals and videos to be broadcast during the events. You can find some of my creations below and find drywet on facebook, soundcloud and bandcamp.
