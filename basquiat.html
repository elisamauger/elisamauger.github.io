JM Basquiat is a school project about an artist of our choice, without contraints we were able to unleash our creativity.
JM Basquiat is a digital library imagined by 3 students, it offers visitors access to a panel of rigorously selected articles, photos and documentaries as well as a soundtrack to be immersed in the NY of the 80's. Through this, we hope to bring a different vision of his life, his work and his commitments. Its design, monochrome and refined, has been thought to be the easiest to use. It allows to highlight the documentary resources that are the main keys of this website. To pay homage to the Prince of the street we used the famous handwriting of Basquiat and his crown drawing which became his emblem.
