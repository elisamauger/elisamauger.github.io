From the first idea to its creation, Next to You is my first big deal in terms of design.
NTY is a two-years' group project, the aim is to put us in the shoes of a start-up launching a new project. We had to imagine and make it from beginning to end. As the main designer in the group, I was able to create my first web design for a website based on my graphic charter. The logo and the design of interactive maps were also part of the challenges taken up on this project. Imagined with pastel colors, eye-catching images and a modern look, it allows visitors to have an intuitive use. The website and the app are still under development but a foretaste is present just below.
